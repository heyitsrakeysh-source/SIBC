Meet Our Founders

Ami Shah

Ami Shah is a sharp-minded Gujarati entrepreneur with a strong track record of scaling businesses. With years of hands-on experience managing finances across multiple restaurant formats and contributing to large business networks like Amway, she brings the vision, discipline, and strategy that power the brand. Her business instincts and passion for innovation helped introduce authentic Benne Dosa and regional South Indian flavours to Mumbai's food scene.

Sudhir Naik

Sudhir Naik, deeply rooted in South Indian culinary traditions, comes with extensive experience in operating and expanding restaurant chains. His expertise in kitchen operations, menu development, and maintaining authentic flavours ensures that every dish stays true to its origins. His commitment to quality and consistency forms the backbone of the brand's food philosophy.

Together

Ami and Sudhir joined forces four years ago, blending her business mastery with his culinary expertise to build one of Mumbai's most loved destinations for South Indian comfort food. Their partnership introduced Benne Dosa to Mumbai and continues to bring regional flavours to the city with authenticity, warmth, and heart.
